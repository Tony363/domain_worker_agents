## BROWSER AUTOMATION INSTRUCTIONS

### Step 1: Navigate to Medical AI Vendor Directories
- **Primary Sources**: HIMSS Healthcare IT Directory, AMIA AI Showcase, FDA 510(k) Database
- **Search Terms**: "clinical decision support AI", "medical imaging AI FDA approved", "EHR analytics machine learning"
- **Action**: Extract vendor names, product categories, and contact information

### Step 2: Extract Tool Specifications
- **Navigate to vendor websites** ‚Üí Product documentation sections
- **Extract**: Accuracy metrics, compliance certifications, integration capabilities
- **Capture**: Screenshots of dashboard interfaces, sample outputs, workflow diagrams

### Step 3: Cross-Reference with FDA 510(k) Database
- **URL**: https://www.accessdata.fda.gov/scripts/cdrh/cfdocs/cfpmn/pmn.cfm
- **Search**: Tool name or vendor company name
- **Extract**: 510(k) clearance number, clearance date, intended use statement, device classification

### Step 4: Scrape Clinical Validation Publications
- **Navigate to PubMed Central**: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/
- **Search Query Format**: "[Tool Name] AND (validation OR clinical trial OR prospective study)"
- **Extract**: PMID, journal name, publication date, study design, sample size, performance metrics
- **Prioritize**: Articles with AUC scores, sensitivity/specificity data, comparison to clinical standard of care

### Step 5: Compare Pricing Models
- **Check vendor pricing pages** for academic institutional licenses
- **Look for**: Per-researcher pricing, departmental licenses, volume discounts
- **Extract**: Base price, per-user costs, implementation fees, support contract costs
- **Note**: Whether pricing is publicly available or requires contact sales
